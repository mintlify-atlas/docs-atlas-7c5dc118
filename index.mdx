## Welcome to OpenPanel

OpenPanel is an open-source analytics platform that gives you the power of Mixpanel with the simplicity of Plausible — and full control over your data. Track events, understand users, replay sessions, and build dashboards, all without sacrificing privacy.

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Sign up at [dashboard.openpanel.dev](https://dashboard.openpanel.dev) and create a new project to get your Client ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install an SDK">
    Add OpenPanel to your app using one of the official SDKs for your platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your first event">
    Call `track()` with an event name and start seeing data in your dashboard within seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
